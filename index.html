Frequently Asked Questions

Students must be 6 years old as of April 1st of the admission year. We require an entrance test in English and Mathematics, and basic reading and number recognition skills.

Annual fees range from ₹5,00,000 to ₹7,75,000 depending on grade level. Fees can be paid in 3 installments: June, September, and January. Sibling discounts of 10% are available. Additional costs include uniform, books, activities, and transport.

Yes, we provide GPS-enabled transport with trained drivers covering multiple routes. Transportation fee varies by zone. All buses have safety features and trained staff. Parents can track buses in real-time through our mobile app.

We follow ICSE and ISC boards for Classes 1-12. IGCSE options are also available for Grade 10 and 12 students. All board curricula emphasize academic excellence and holistic development.

We offer cricket, badminton, basketball, tennis, volleyball, and athletics. Extracurricular activities include music, dance, drama, visual arts, coding, robotics, debates, and social service programs. Participation is compulsory for character building.

Campus is secured with CCTV surveillance, trained security staff, and restricted entry. We have emergency protocols, first-aid facilities, and regular safety drills. All staff undergo background verification and training.

We have interactive smart classrooms, digital learning platforms, coding labs, and AI/robotics programs. Every classroom has smart boards and online learning management system. Students learn digital literacy and cybersecurity.

We have a dedicated inclusion team for students with learning differences. Individualized Education Plans (IEPs) are created. We offer speech therapy, occupational therapy, and counseling services. Teacher assistants are available in classrooms.

Our Class 12 students achieve 98% university placement. Students get admitted to top Indian colleges and international universities including IITs, Delhi University, Mumbai University, and overseas institutions. Average scholarship rate: 35%.

We conduct parent-teacher meetings twice a year. Regular progress reports are shared through parent portal every term. Urgent issues are communicated immediately. Weekly newsletters update families about school events and academic progress.
